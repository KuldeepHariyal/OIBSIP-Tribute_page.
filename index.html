Virat Kohli.

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and the leader of team Indian national cricket team who plays for RCB(Royal challengers bangalore) in IPl and for delhi in Indian domestic cricket.

Well known as

  • King Kohli.
  • Run Machine Kohli.
  • Chase master.

    • About Virat Kohli

    • Born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi.
    • Kohli trained at the west Delhi Cricket Academy and started his youth career with delhi under-15 team.
    • After making an impression at the youth level virat selected for India under-19 team.
    • In Malaysia's under-19 world cup, virat lead team India to win and finshed the competition as one of the top 3 run scorer.
    • He made his International debut in 2008 and quickly become key player in the ODI team and later made his test debut in 2011.
    • RCB signed(Ipl team) virat in year 2008.
    • In 2013 Kohli reached the number one spot in ICC rankings for ODI batsmen for the first time.
    • During 2014 T20 world cup, he set record for the most run scored in the tournament.
    • In 2018, he achieved yet another milestone, becoming the world's top ranked Test batsmen, making him the only Indian cricketer to hold the number one spot in all three formate of the game.
    • He has received many accolades for his performances on the cricket field
    • At the national level, Kohli was honoured with the Arjuna Award in 2013, the Padma Shri under the sports category in 2017 and the Rajiv Gandhi khel Ratana Award, India's highest sporting honour, in 2018.
